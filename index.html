About Vidyotan

"Vidyotan" is the annual Science and Technology based National level activity of Sipna College of Engineering and Technology, Amravati. The sole purpose of organizing this Techfest event is to give the students a platform to compete from all around to showcase their talent and skills in various integrated techno events. These events promote a real technical trade for the budding engineers to come up with their bright ideas.

This year the Vidyotan theme is Chandrayaan-3
Exploring The Universe.

It is organized on the 8th Feb 2024.
